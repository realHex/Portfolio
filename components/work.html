Online Middleman

Experience: 3 years



A middleman refers to a person who makes sure a trade/sale happens safely and smoothly between two parties.

People trade/sell things such as Social media accounts, Ingame accounts, Ingame items online. But it's difficult for two people to do that safely, since it allows one side to scam the other. That's where I come in.



My job as a middleman was to make sure that both parties received their end of the trade safely and fairly. This meant that I had to be a trusted person among the community, which was difficult at first, but as I did more and more trades, the people who I helped complete trades successfully vouched for me and recommended me to others.

I help people complete their trades, and in return they vouch and pay me.

I have been a middleman for over 3 years and have completed over 300 successful trades, with over 600 people vouching for me.

Things I had to do as a middleman

  • If it was a social/ingame account, I had to login to that said account and take screenshots so that the buyer can confirm that it was infact the correct item/account.

  • I would then take the item from the seller, and keep it in my possession securely. Depending on the item, this involved checking if it was the correct item, changing the linked email address, changing passwords, adding 2FA etc. This was done so that there was no way for the seller to get the item back.

  • Once both parties confirmed that they were satisfied with the trade, I would give the item to the buyer and the payment to the seller.

  • This was a risky business, since one wrong move from my side and it could lead to a potential scam.

  • I also had to make sure to write down the names of the people whom I helped trade, their ids. I also screenrecorded the whole trade process so that if there was a dispute, I could show the video as proof.

  • Doing this job meant I had to constantly interact with different kinds of people. Which was good at times, and not so good at other times. Nevertheless, it did teach me a lot of things about people and how to deal with them. It also taught me a lot about being patient and how to handle stressful situations. Almost like a Customer Service job.
