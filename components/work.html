My YouTube Channel: Hexun

I created my Youtube channel when I was 11 years old (2016). I always loved making content and sharing stuff that I knew, so editing was really fun for me. I mostly made gaming videos, tutorials and editing packs (so that other people can use them when they are editing).

I have stopped editing currently, but I do plan on returning sometime very soon :)


Fun facts about my channel

  • My most popular video was on "How to get unlimited coins and gems in Shadow Fight 2" with over 270k views
  • My First video was "How to use Xpadder on Windows 10" published on 21st January, 2016.
  • Over 10000 people have downloaded my editing packs

Middleman Project

The Middleman project represents my efforts to bridge the gap between complex technologies and everyday users. This initiative focuses on simplifying technical concepts and making them accessible to everyone.

Through carefully crafted content and innovative approaches, Middleman serves as an interpreter between technical jargon and practical understanding, helping users navigate the increasingly complex digital landscape with confidence.
